Rocky and Rockette

Rocko's was founded by Rocko and his wife, Rockette, after coming back from an outdoor rock climbing adventure in the Rockies. They realized that not everyone should have to travel to a mountain to experience the joy of rock climbing, and decided to create a space welcoming of all that would make that a reality!

Visit us at the gym, located at:
123 Rock Road
Castle Rock, CO 11111
USA

Warning! Rock climbing is a fun activity if proper safety precautions are followed (which we will teach and help you with). Please be sure to follow all verbal and posted safety precautions for yours and others' safety!
